Summer Brand

Established in 2011, Anh Duong Distribution quickly affirmed its pioneering position in the field of distributing mother and baby products of many famous brands in the world. Our partners come from the US, France, Germany, Japan and Korea, these are countries that have very strict requirements in the production process as well as the quality of exported products. Therefore, the brands have all won prestigious awards, recognized by thousands of parents and many international organizations.

  • 2012: Become the exclusive distributor in Vietnam of Bornfree, Summer Infant, Pura (USA) brands.
  • 2014: Become the exclusive distributor in Vietnam of the brands Richell (Japan) and Comotomo (USA).
  • 2015: Established SHC Trading Investment Joint Stock Company - a unit under Anh Duong Distribution. Expand distribution market to Laos and Cambodia.
  • 2016: Exclusive distribution in Vietnam 2 brands Babymoov (France) and Mother-K&K-Mom (Korea) by SHC.
  • 2018: Become the exclusive distributor in Vietnam of the brand NUK (Germany).
Currently, Anh Duong Distribution has built a large supply system, stretching from the North to the South with more than 1000 large and small agents and reputable mother and baby supermarket chains such as: AEON, Bibomart, Children's Shop, Kidsplaza , Tuticare, SNB, Adayroi, Babyland & Toyland… Sunshine Distribution orients to develop an enthusiastic sales and customer care team to bring customers the best and most reliable service.
